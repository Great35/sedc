Terms and Conditions of Participation:

Eligibility and rules of origin of Participation: All our tender supply projects are open to all legal persons (participating either individually or in a group (consortium) of tenderers, foreign companies) which are established in any country or territory of the regions covered and/or authorized by the specific instruments applicable to the program under which the contract is financed. All products supplied under this contract must be of good quality and in compliance with standard.

Company Registration:

Every tenderer/supplying company must have a local agent as a representative. All interested companies who wish to be part of our tender supply project must be registered as one of our legal contractors/suppliers to the South East Development Commission.

Tender Document:

The following documents are required to be submitted by suppliers as their contract supply documentation:

  • A quotation or proforma invoice covering the products to be supplied
  • A letter of intent stating the supplier's ability to handle the contract
  • A letter authorizing and appointing a local company as agent/representative for the tender process
  • Completed copy of the registration form
  • Proof of payment for the registration fee
  • Certificate of standard for the product to be supplied (if any)

Destination Port:

Apapa Sea Port, Lagos, Nigeria

Language of the Contract:

All written communications for this tender procedure and contract must be in English.
All contract supply documents must be addressed to:
Office of the Chairman
Contract Awarding Committee
South East Development Commission
NO. 28 Garden Ave, GRA, Enugu 400102, Enugu,Nigeria.

Supply/Delivery Duration:

The tender supply duration is 1-12 months after the supplier's confirmation of full payment, and partial shipment/delivery is allowed.
